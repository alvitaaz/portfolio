Procurement & Import Intern

During my internship in the Purchasing Division, specifically in Procurement Import at PT Toppan Plasindo Lestari, I was involved in managing import documentation and supporting the administrative process of incoming goods. I worked cross-functionally with the warehouse and finance teams to ensure smooth and compliant procurement operations. In addition, I actively monitored the arrival of goods by coordinating with warehouse staff to ensure that deliveries were received on time and that all supporting documents were properly in place.

Documentation of Import Tasks

During the import process, I was responsible for managing and verifying several important documents to ensure compliance with customs regulations and to support a smooth shipping workflow. Below are some of the key documents that I frequently managed and verified during the import process:

  • Invoice: Contains details of the billing issued by the supplier for the goods purchased.
  • Packing List: Includes contents, weight, and packaging details of the shipment.
  • Bill of Lading: Issued by the shipper as proof of delivery and details of the cargo.
  • Insurance Certificate: Verified to ensure the shipment was properly covered, ensuring protection against damage or loss during transit.
  • Certificate of Origin (COO): Verifies the country where the goods were produced.
  • PIB (Import Declaration): Customs documents required for import clearance.
